"Hidden History: Syracuse 8" by Jennifer Sanders

In this Emmy-nominated documentary, Jennifer Sanders highlights a group of living legends who sacrificed almost everything for the sake of equality.
The Syracuse 8, a group of African-American football players at Syracuse University in the late 1960s, protested inequality and racism through a boycott that made national headlines.
This documentary aired on NewsChannel 9, WSYR-TV.

The Syracuse 8: Then, Now and Forever Orange

This year (Sept, 2020), Greg Allen and Joe Ehrmann returned to Syracuse University (via Zoom) to have a moderated discussion with Vera Jones. In recognition of the 50th Anniversary of the Syracuse 8 Protests, the Office of Multicultural Advancement and Syracuse Athletics present a virtual program, The Syracuse 8: Then, Now and Forever Orange.
